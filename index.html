About Our Company

Shourya Infra Solar Solutions is a renewable energy installation company dedicated to helping homes and businesses transition to clean electricity. We design and install rooftop solar systems tailored to individual energy consumption and building structure.

Our team manages the complete process including inspection, system design, installation, testing, and long-term maintenance. Our goal is to make solar adoption simple and reliable for every customer.

By adopting solar power, clients reduce electricity costs while contributing to environmental sustainability and energy independence.

Our Work

Our Installation Process

Site Inspection

Our technicians visit the location to evaluate the roof structure, shading, orientation, and electrical infrastructure to determine feasibility and layout.

We take exact measurements, note potential obstructions, and discuss any customer concerns during the walkthrough.

The condition of the roof is checked to ensure it can support the panel load, and any repairs are recommended before installation.

We examine access points for equipment and verify local code requirements with municipal authorities.

Our survey includes photographing the site from multiple angles to aid in later design stages.

We also inspect the electrical panel and meter location to plan wiring runs and inverter placement.

Client preferences for panel placement and orientation are discussed to align with aesthetic concerns.

Potential future expansion scenarios are considered during this assessment.

A detailed report is prepared summarizing findings, recommendations, and next steps.

Once the inspection is complete, we schedule the energy analysis phase based on collected data.

Energy Analysis

We review historical electricity usage and perform load calculations to size the system appropriately to meet your needs.

Simulation software estimates production versus consumption, ensuring cost savings and ROI goals are achievable.

Seasonal variations are taken into account to ensure year-round reliability.

We analyze peak demand periods and design the system to offset the most expensive usage.

Future changes in energy habits, such as EV charging or new appliances, are projected.

Shading analysis using specialized tools helps optimize panel placement.

We model system performance using local weather data for accurate predictions.

Financial projections include incentives, tax credits, and payback timelines.

Customers receive a comprehensive energy report with visual charts and recommendations.

Once analysis is approved, we advance to developing the system design package.

System Design

A custom solar system layout is created, selecting panels, inverters, and mounting based on site conditions and energy goals.

We provide detailed schematics and work with electricians to integrate with your existing meter and grid connection.

Designs include structural calculations for racking and anchoring based on roof type.

Electrical one-lines are prepared showing wiring, inverter location, and disconnects.

We select components from reputable manufacturers ensuring warranties and compatibility.

Permit drawings are produced to satisfy local authority requirements.

We plan conduit runs and equipment placement to minimize visual impact.

Energy storage options, such as battery backup, are discussed if desired.

Clients review and approve design; any revisions are incorporated before ordering materials.

After design approval, we finalize a timeline and procurement list to begin installation.

Installation

Our certified crew installs racking, panels, and wiring following safety standards and manufacturer instructions.

Equipment is inspected on delivery and we coordinate with building inspectors for permits as required.

Racking is secured to roof trusses with corrosion-resistant fasteners.

Panels are lifted into place using safe handling practices and leveled precisely.

All wiring is run in conduit with proper weatherproof fittings.

Grounding and bonding are performed to code to ensure electrical safety.

Inverter and metering equipment are mounted in accessible locations.

We maintain a clean site, removing packaging and debris daily.

Quality checks are completed at each stage to catch any issues early.

Once the array is in place, we schedule final inspections and testing.

Maintenance Support

Ongoing monitoring and service are provided to ensure long-term efficiency and address any issues promptly.

We offer regular inspections, inverter firmware updates, and panel cleaning recommendations to keep output high over decades.

Performance reports are generated monthly and sent to the customer.

Any drop in production triggers an alert and immediate investigation.

We coordinate with manufacturers for warranty service if components fail.

Routine visual inspections check for debris, damage, or animal nesting.

Inverters are checked for proper ventilation and cleaned if necessary.

We advise on shading changes (new trees, construction) that could impact performance.

Education is provided on safe panel cleaning techniques to customers.

A long-term maintenance plan is available to extend system life.
